About Us

Who We Are

Suchitra Industries is a specialized Non-Woven Products Manufacturing Company located in Hyderabad, Telangana. We work with enterprises in the Trading, Services, Manufacturing, Government and Technology domains offering business specific solutions and services that are responsive and cost-effective. With the benefit of local understanding and insight, we provide products & services that are relevant, practical and useful for the demand of today's small, medium and large sized businesses for their packing and promotional needs.

What We Do

Suchitra was started with an intention to promote eco-friendly products. Nonwoven Fabric Bag is one of our flagship products manufactured with dexterity of art and technical skills with a range of bags of varied sizes, colors, shapes and thickness to suit to the specific needs of its customers. Our designing and printing services cater for customers to make their brands stand out well in the market. We have nonwoven bags that are ready to deliver, ready to print and customizable.

Why Suchitra

A combination of our in-depth product knowledge and our design and technical skills ensure high quality products delivered to businesses across different verticals.

Very unique about our products is that they are Ecofriendly. Being non-toxic, recyclable and reusable they do great favor to keep the environment clean and green. Manufactured with state-of-art machinery and quality raw materials, our products are long-lasting, qualitative and reliable with multiple usage.

Infrastructure

Our infrastructure consists of state of art production facility, trained and dedicated professionals, quality assessment department and other divisions for designing, printing and delivery. Our quality program ensures that our products meet the standard quality norms right from procuring raw materials, production process till delivery.

Our manufacturing unit comprises of advanced nonwoven bag making machines, sewing machines, slitting machines, Flexo printing machine, screen printing set up and several other supporting machines using latest technology. We also have excellent warehouse facility, connected with best transportation mediums, instrumental for our quick order execution.
